MG 2011 - Reno, NV










“MG 2011 – A Western Experience” was held June 13 to 17, 2011
at the “Tri-Properties” (Silver Legacy, Circus-Circus and El Dorado, Hotels & Casinos) in downtown Reno, NV.
This was the fourth all MG register event that had been held every 5th year in North America since 1996.
The event was sponsored by the five national MG registers who make up North American Council of MG Registers.
